# Jungle Jambu EP1 — Jambu and the Wild Boar

**Story outline (English) — Pipeline converts to Tanglish in Stage 2**

Scene 1 – Narrator
Every morning, Jungle Jambu sat outside his hut with a big hat and broken binoculars. He proudly told everyone, "Listen carefully! I am the greatest hunter in this forest. No animal can escape from Jungle Jambu!"

Scene 2 – Villager
"Jambu! Please help us! A huge wild boar has come into the market. It is eating all the vegetables and everyone is too scared to go near it!"

Scene 3 – Jungle Jambu
"A wild boar? Is that all? Don't worry at all. I, Jungle Jambu, will catch that boar in just five minutes. You all wait here and watch!"

Scene 4 – Narrator
Jambu walked bravely toward the market. But the moment he saw the boar, his face changed. The boar was enormous, with sharp tusks and angry eyes.

Scene 5 – Jungle Jambu
"Ayyo… this is not an ordinary boar. This looks like a giant monster! Maybe… maybe I should come back tomorrow instead…"

Scene 6 – Narrator
Just then, the boar saw Jambu and started running toward him. Jambu screamed loudly and ran through the jungle faster than he had ever run before.

Scene 7 – Narrator
While running, Jambu crashed straight into a tree. Suddenly, a huge beehive fell from the branch and landed right on the boar's head. Angry bees flew everywhere and chased the boar far away.

Scene 8 – Villager
"Amazing! Did you see that? Jungle Jambu used the bees to chase away the boar. What a clever plan! He really is the greatest hunter in the forest!"

Scene 9 – Jungle Jambu
Jambu stood up slowly, covered in mud and leaves. He fixed his hat and said proudly, "Of course! That was my secret jungle trick!" Then he saw the bees flying toward him and shouted, "Ayyo! My secret trick is chasing me now!"
