## The Strategy (3 Pillars)

### Pillar 1: Shorts Factory (Algorithm Growth)
**Target: 3–4 Shorts/week minimum**
- Post at 3–5 PM UAE time consistently
- Seed every Short in Tamil community groups within the **first hour** of posting (passes YouTube's Layer 1 algorithm test)
- Short format: jump straight to action/emotion — no intros, no title cards
- Rotate types: funny scene → cultural hook → emotional moment → challenge/interactive → BTS
- Optimal length: 30–55 seconds
- Add Tamil + English captions manually (YouTube auto-captions Tamil poorly)

**Expected: 2,000–5,000 subs from algorithm alone over 90 days if 1 Short/day**

### Pillar 2: Community Seeding (Diaspora Outreach)

**Budget: £150 total, Facebook/Instagram only (NOT YouTube ads)**

- Objective: Video views (not subs — cheaper CPM, drives organic)
- Kill switch: Stop if CTR < 0.5% after £50 test spend

**Expected: 300–600 subs for £150**

## Thumbnail + Title Formula

**Thumbnail:**
- Character face front-and-center (60–70% of frame)
- Single dominant emotion (surprise/joy/wonder)
- Bright saturated background (yellow/orange/red > blue/green)
- Max 4 words of bold text, readable on mobile
- One character, one emotion, no clutter

**Title formula:**
`[Character]'s [Adventure/Story]! [emoji] | Tamil Kids Story | Tiny Tamil Tales`
Example: `Kavin's Rainy Day Adventure! 🌧️ | Tamil Kids Story | Tiny Tamil Tales`

**Tags:** Always include: tamil kids story, tamil animation, tamil kids, tamil stories for children, tiny tamil tales + episode-specific tags

**English subtitles on every video** — zero Tamil kids channels do this. Instant diaspora differentiator.

## Pivot Decision Tree

```
After 2 weeks of daily Shorts:
  → Avg views < 100/Short?
     Fix: Change hook. First 3 seconds must be action/sound/emotion.
     Try: Cut to under 30 seconds, start mid-story.

  → 100–500 views/Short?
     Normal. Stay course. Identify best performer, make 3 more like it.

  → One Short hits 1,000+ views?
     DOUBLE DOWN. Make 5 more with identical format/topic.
     Reply to every comment. Run £50 Facebook ad on that specific Short.

Community outreach — post gets removed/no traction?
  → Wrong framing. Post as a parent sharing, not as the channel.

Subs stuck for 2 weeks?
  → Check: End screens on all long-form? Subscribe CTAs in Short descriptions?
  → Nuclear option: Email all UK Tamil schools in same week.

Paid ads burning money (CTR < 0.5% after £50)?
  → Kill. Change creative. Try emotional over funny. Add bilingual text.
```
